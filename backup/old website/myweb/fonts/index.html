About TP

TECHNO PARV, or Techfest, as it is fondly called, the technical & cultural festival of IPS Gwalior. It is held in the mid of every year and attracts more than 5,000 students from colleges and schools all over the INDIA.

It was started in 2007 by a bunch of enthusiastic IPSians. Techno parv has now snowballed to become the largest festival of its kind in this Region.

Techno Parv has been the trendsetter for various technical and Art Venture festivals in Gwalior. This year, your favourite fest is going to be more innovative, more exciting, more creative, more enjoying and awesome than ever.

BEST 4 DAYS OF YOUR LIFE

A WORLD CLASS ORGANIZATION
